How MyCloset Works

1. Make an Account and Sign-In

Once you've signed up for MyCloset, you can easily look at the contents of your closet anywhere! Simply log-in with your email and password on the main page.

2. Add or Remove items

Once you're logged in to your account, click the "add" or "remove" buttons to get started! Anytime you add something to your wardrobe, whether it's that slick new top or just a new addition to the household you want to keep tabs on, simply input the item name, price, and purchase date (MM/DD/YYYY). Voila! It's in your closet. To empty items from your closet, check the boxes next to all the items you want to remove then click the "Retire Items" button.

3. Donate

Once you've decided to throw something out, MyCloset makes it easy to see how much you have left and what you've retired from the closet- you should see the total value and number of items in your closet update. You'll also see your list of retired/donated items grow on the right side of the page. If you want to donate items, just click the "Find Donation Centers Near Me" button for some suggestions on where to drop off your stuff!
